About Lulu's Portfolio

As a student of design and illustration, I've explored a wide variety of projects, media, and styles. The majority of my experience, through school and freelance work, has been in layout, branding, and frontend production art and development for mobile and web.

My true passions, in terms of visual art, lie in spot illustration and storytelling through a marriage of images and prose—an approach I consider less graphic novel and more "illustrative novel."

The work in this gallery includes everything from both my academic and professional careers that showcases either a "finished" level of execution or a crucial step in the design process. For more sketches and works in progress, feel free to check out my Tumblr. For my technical projects, pop over to my GitHub.
