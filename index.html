Who am I?

Hello, my name is Lulu and I am a professional generalist. I write fiction, draw pictures, design software, and champion excellent user experience.

My philosophy: design should delight, everything should be designed, and all design should be excecuted with unparalleled craft.

The second most important thing in my life (after craft) is a lifelong devotion to learning. As much as I value collaboration, I love knowing everything about how to execute a design all by myself. New problems excite me because it means I get to learn something in order to solve it.

I also love using typewriters and roller skating. I'm researching node.js in order to build a browser-based MUSH. I have a B.Sc. in Computer Science, but I'm also graduating from design school this spring with an emphasis on illustration.
